Perfect Steak Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 steaks (ribeye, sirloin, or your favorite cut)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2-3 sprigs fresh thyme or rosemary
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Season both sides of the steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until smoking hot. Add olive oil.
  4. Place the steaks in the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust for your desired doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add butter, garlic, and herbs to the skillet. Spoon the melted butter over the steaks for added flavor.
  6. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serve your steak with your favorite sides and enjoy!
