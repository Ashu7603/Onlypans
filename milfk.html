Classic Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream and a cherry for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine ice cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Adjust the thickness by adding more milk if necessary.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  4. Top with whipped cream and a cherry if desired.
  5. Serve immediately with a straw and enjoy!
